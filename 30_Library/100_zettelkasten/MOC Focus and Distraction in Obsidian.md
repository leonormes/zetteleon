[[Maintaining focus is a core challenge of knowledge work]]. This challenge can be amplified by our tools. [[Powerful digital tools can be major sources of distraction]], offering endless opportunities for non-essential tasks. A common failure mode is engaging in [[Tool tinkering is a form of productive procrastination]], where we work on the system instead of within it. This is particularly damaging because The friction of organising can break a flow state, meaning even well-intentioned interruptions can derail deep thinking.
