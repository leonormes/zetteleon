## PINCH Model for ADHD Motivation

**Summary:** Five key triggers that effectively engage ADHD brains:

1. **P**assion/Play
2. **I**nterest
3. **N**ovelty
4. **C**hallenge
5. **H**urry (Urgency)

**Neurological Basis:**
Each component stimulates dopamine production
Bypasses typical dysregulation
Creates sustainable engagement
