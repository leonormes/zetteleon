ADHD thrives on complexity because it gives you endless ways to tweak instead of commit.

- One note = One thought. No structure, no tags, no links at first. Just:
  - What’s the idea? (1-2 sentences)
  - Why does it matter? (1-2 sentences)
- Example:

> "Note: Banach-Tarski Paradox says you can split a sphere into 5 pieces and reassemble them into two identical spheres. Why cool: It breaks intuition about volume, showing math can ‘create’ space from nothing."
