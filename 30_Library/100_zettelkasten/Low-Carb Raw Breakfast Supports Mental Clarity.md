A nutrient-dense, raw, and low-carbohydrate breakfast is optimal for mental clarity. Processed foods and high-carb meals can lead to a "food coma," while a breakfast of raw vegetables, protein, and healthy fats supports sustained energy and focus. If carbohydrates are consumed, exposure to daylight (blue light) can help counter sleepiness by suppressing melatonin.
