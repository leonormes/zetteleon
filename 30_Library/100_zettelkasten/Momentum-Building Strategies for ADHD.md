## Momentum-Building Strategies for ADHD

For the ADHD brain, overcoming inertia is often the most difficult part of any task. Momentum-building strategies are designed to create forward motion with the least possible resistance.

### Core Principle

The fundamental idea behind these strategies is that motivation is not a prerequisite for action, but a result of it. By starting with small, manageable tasks, one can generate the momentum needed to tackle larger challenges.

These are specific, practical methods to lower the activation energy required to start a task.
