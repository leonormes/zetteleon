## Sequence - Evolution of Fable Storytelling

This sequence traces the historical development of the fable as a storytelling form, from its ancient oral roots to its adaptation in modern media.

2.  **Literary Adaptation**: [[Phaedrus Adapted Aesop's Fables into Latin Verse|Phaedrus' Latin versions (1st century CE)]]
3.  **Courtly Education**: [[La Fontaines Fables Served as Courtly Education in 17th Century France|La Fontaine's French fables (17th century)]]
4.  **Visual Storytelling**: [[The Versailles Labyrinth Used Fable Installations for Visual Storytelling|Versailles labyrinth installations]]
5.  **Modern Media**: [[Fables in Modern Media Range from Cartoons to Political Allegory|Cartoons (1920s) to Animal Farm (1945)]]
