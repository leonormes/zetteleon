When you've spent a great deal of time developing an idea in the privacy of your own mind, it's perfectly formed and coherent to you. Exposing it to someone else is a vulnerable act. If their immediate reaction isn't what you hoped for, or if they misunderstand, it can feel jarring and disproportionately impactful because they are the first external point of contact for this carefully constructed thought.
