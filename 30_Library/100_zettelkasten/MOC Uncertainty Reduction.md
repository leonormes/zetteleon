[[The World's Ambiguity Drives the Need for Certainty]]. To manage this, we often seek external validation. This process works because [[External Validation Reduces Cognitive Dissonance]], which is the discomfort of holding conflicting beliefs. The resulting feeling is powerful, often perceived as a [[Confirmation as a Perceived Shortcut to Truth]].
