Timeboxing adds a critical commitment of achieving a specified outcome within a fixed period, unlike time blocking which just reserves time. This creates urgency and propels work forward.

Timeboxing to Combat Procrastination, Perfectionism, and Parkinson's Law: Timeboxing helps overcome procrastination by breaking down large projects into manageable slots, combats perfectionism by prompting movement after the timer ends, and mitigates Parkinson's Law by imposing fixed constraints.
