## Cognitive Health Practices for Technical Work

**Summary:** Evidence-based habits to sustain mental performance:

1. **Exercise:**
   - Boosts BDNF ("neural fertilizer")
   - Short bouts effective

2. **Sleep:**
   - Review tough problems before bed
   - Enables memory consolidation

3. **Nutrition:**
   - Nuts, cruciferous vegetables
   - Dark chocolate flavonoids
