- Researching the "perfect" productivity system instead of using any system.
- Outlining dozens of blog posts instead of writing one.
- Creating elaborate workout plans instead of exercising.
- Organizing tools and a workspace instead of starting the actual work.
- Reading about a new skill instead of actively practicing it.
- Networking and having coffee meetings without ever sending a proposal or making a direct ask.
- Brainstorming ideas for a project without ever starting to build it.
