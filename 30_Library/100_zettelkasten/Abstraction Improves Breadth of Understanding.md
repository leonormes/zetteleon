To overcome the limitations of finite cognitive capacity and myopic understanding, resorting to higher levels of abstraction is beneficial. By simplifying complex details into broader concepts, individuals can gain a more expansive view and grasp more information at once.

## Core Relationships

The Problem: [[Human Understanding is Inherently Myopic]] due to our [[Limited Human Information Processing Capacity]]

The Solution: [[Abstraction as Climbing a Hill]] - Higher abstraction provides a wider view, like ascending for better perspective

The Mechanism: This connects to how [[Understanding Enables Accurate Predictions]] and [[Understanding Reveals Cause-and-Effect]] - broader context improves both

The Outcome: [[Broader Understanding Enhances Predictive & Causal Insight]] through the expanded informational context that abstraction provides

## Related Cognitive Concepts

- [[Illusion of Explanatory Depth (IoED)]] - Abstraction helps distinguish real understanding from surface familiarity
- [[Working Memory Challenges in Technical Context]] - Abstraction reduces cognitive load by chunking information
- [[The Extended Mind Thesis]] - External tools can help manage abstraction layers
- [[Metacognitive Awareness]] - Conscious monitoring of your abstraction level

## Practical Applications

- [[Brain Activity During Programming Tasks]] - How abstraction affects neural processing in technical work
- [[Thinking & Creating Skills (Your Clever Brain)]] - Perspective/wisdom as seeing the "big picture"

## Tensions and Limitations

- [[Deep Focus on a Single Concept Sparks Innovation]] - Sometimes depth trumps breadth
- [[Discomfort with Ambiguity Prevents Deeper Thinking]] - Abstraction can create uncomfortable uncertainty
