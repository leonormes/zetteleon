- Definition: Continuing investment based on past costs rather than future prospects
- Software Context:
  - Maintaining legacy systems beyond reasonable cost
  - Continuing failed architectural approaches
  - Refusing to abandon problematic frameworks
- Example: "We've already spent a year on this approach, we have to continue"
