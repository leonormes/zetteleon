Understanding the challenges of ADHD requires looking at the role of metacognition. There is a fundamental link whereby [[ADHD is Associated with Deficits in Metacognition]]. This is not an abstract concept; these [[Metacognitive Deficits in ADHD Create Practical Impairments]], affecting a person's ability to understand their own motivation, devise strategies, and assess their performance accurately.
