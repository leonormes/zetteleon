## 7.10 ENI Usage in Multi-AZ Deployments

### Key Points

- ENIs are critical in multi-AZ architectures to ensure high availability of workloads.
- Traffic can be rerouted between ENIs across Availability Zones to ensure continuity in case of failure in one AZ.
