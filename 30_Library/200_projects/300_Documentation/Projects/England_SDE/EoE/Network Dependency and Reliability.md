The FITFILE cluster's ability to function and retrieve data becomes dependent on the continuous availability and performance of the ExpressRoute connection and the on-premises database. Any network issues or downtime on the on-premises side will directly impact FITFILE. Having the data local in Azure reduces this dependency.
