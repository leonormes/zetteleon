## Give Me an Aerosmith Discography in Chronological order

Here is the chronological discography of Aerosmith's studio albums:

- Aerosmith (1973)
- Get Your Wings (1974)
- Toys in the Attic (1975)
- Rocks (1976)
- Draw the Line (1977)
- Night in the Ruts (1979)
- Rock in a Hard Place (1982)
- Done with Mirrors (1985)
- Permanent Vacation (1987)
- Pump (1989)
- Get a Grip (1993)
- Nine Lives (1997)
- Just Push Play (2001)
- Honkin' on Bobo (2004)
- Music from Another Dimension! (2012)

This list covers their main studio albums, from their debut in 1973 up to 2012. Aerosmith has also released numerous live albums, compilation albums, and extended plays in addition to these studio albums.
