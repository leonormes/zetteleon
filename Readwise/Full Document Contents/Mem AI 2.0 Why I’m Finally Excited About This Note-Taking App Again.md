## Full Document
One of the applications I have been most excited about this year has been [Mem AI](https://mem.ai/). Mem is actually starting to be one of the older applications in the productivity market. It was one of the first applications that sold the idea of AI in our productivity apps. It wanted to be the self-organizing workspace, and the idea of Mem has always been amazing. People loved it, and more and more people moved to Mem when it first launched.

![](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:700/1*WoUhTsVakZGq33Jw9AT83g.png)
However, even though the idea was amazing, there was a huge problem with the execution. The app simply did not work as it was supposed to. With bugs, issues, and no clear vision of what Mem was trying to become, people started to move away. After the launch of ChatGPT and LLMs, more applications started doing a lot of the same things as Mem. People found great alternatives to Mem, and in the end, it resulted in Mem starting over and building Mem 2.0.

![](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:700/1*7LrSP2sRL5LBEkYhy_69Ag.png)
I am really excited about Mem 2.0. If Mem is able to deliver, it will be the best AI note-taking app, or at least one of them. It has done a lot of things right, but the bugs combined with the fact that Mem had different versions across devices and platforms made it…
