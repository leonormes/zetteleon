## Core Principles & Values (Horizon 5)

This document represents Horizon 5, the highest level of the Horizons of Focus model. It is the personal constitution that defines the "why" behind every goal, project, and action within the ProdOS system. These principles are the ultimate criteria for decision-making and the source of intrinsic motivation.

The process of defining these principles is one of deep self-reflection, centered on **Habit 2: Begin with the End in Mind**. It involves asking what you want your legacy to be in terms of **Character** (who you want to be) and **Contribution** (what you want to do).

### The Six Virtues of a Flourishing Life

The core principles are structured around six universal virtues that form the pillars of a well-lived life.

#### II. The Virtue of Courage

*The emotional strengths for accomplishing goals despite opposition.*

- **My Principle: Pursue Systemic Impact (Walk Upstream) üåä**
  I choose the courage to address root causes rather than the comfort of managing symptoms. I reject the ceaseless labor of "moving buckets under leaks" and instead dedicate my energy to "fixing the leak at its source." My most fulfilling work is that which acts as a force multiplier, creating cascading, positive change.
- **Guideline:** Is this a reactive fix, or does it address a root cause?

  My core belief is that people are capable and do their best work when they feel valued, heard, and psychologically secure. I practice genuine empathy and compassion as the primary tools for building meaningful collaborations and fostering an environment of trust.

  My private character and public actions must be one and the same. Honesty, authenticity, and transparency are non-negotiable. I feel a deep sense of shame from any hint of fakeness or deceit, as it fractures my sense of wholeness.
- **Guideline:** Is this action honest and authentic? Is it fair to all involved?

#### V. The Virtue of Temperance

*The strengths that protect against excess and impulsiveness.*

- **My Principle: Practice Compassionate Self-Awareness üôè**
  I temper my reactions with mindfulness and self-reflection. I understand that all humans are prone to cognitive biases, and I commit to understanding my own internal state without judgment. I take responsibility for my actions without assigning blame, choosing forgiveness over grievance.
- **Guideline:** Am I reacting, or am I consciously choosing my response?

#### VI. The Virtue of Transcendence

*The strengths that forge connections to the larger universe and create meaning.*

- **My Principle: Create Meaning from Gratitude and Joy ‚ú®**

- **Guideline:** Does this path lead towards more joy? Can I find something to be grateful for right now?

These principles form the "true north" for the ProdOS system. They are reviewed during the Weekly Review to ensure that all goals, projects, and actions remain in alignment with this deeply held personal constitution.
