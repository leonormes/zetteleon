**Primary Concern:** Anxiety mentioned by parents and school staff as key issue
**Family Context:** Bessie's sister also has anxiety

## Anxiety Manifestations

### Physical Symptoms

- Feeling unwell when anxious (parents link behavior to anxiety)
- "Psychosomatic element" noted in school reports
- Difficulty sitting still during assessments

### Behavioral Responses

- Using time-out pass to access quieter spaces
- Walking out of lessons when overwhelmed
- Refusing to return to class after using time-out card
- Meltdowns when unable to self-regulate

### Academic Impact

- Anxiety about math grouping and timetabling
- Overwhelm from large information volumes
- Difficulty with sustained focus when anxious
- Struggles with copying from board due to stress

## Current Support Strategies

### School Interventions

- **Time-out card:** 2-minute time-out for self-regulation
- **Counselling sessions:** Weekly sessions starting May 2025
- **Access to sensory room:** Quiet space for regulation
- **Breathing techniques:** For relaxation support

### Identified Gaps

- 5-minute anxiety breaks insufficient without guided support
- Inconsistent understanding of anxiety triggers across staff
- Punitive responses to anxiety-driven behaviors
