Weekly or bi-weekly cleaning tasks are for addressing high-traffic areas that need more than a quick daily tidy. This typically includes deep cleaning kitchens and bathrooms, doing laundry for sheets and towels, dusting and vacuuming open spaces, and emptying garbage bins.
