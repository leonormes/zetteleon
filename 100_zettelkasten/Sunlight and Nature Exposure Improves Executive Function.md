Spending at least 15 minutes in an environment with sunlight and greenery has been shown to improve executive function, boost mood, and lower stress. For individuals with ADHD, nature exposure is linked to better outcomes. This can be as simple as sitting near a window with a view of trees or spending time on a patio or in a park.
