Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria (RSD) is an intense emotional response to perceived criticism or rejection, commonly experienced by people with ADHD. This heightened sensitivity can make interactions with authority figures particularly challenging and can contribute to a complex relationship with power dynamics in both directions - both receiving and giving authority.
