## Moral Encyclopedia Concept in Storytelling

**Summary:** Stories function as repositories of cultural wisdom by:
- Encoding ethics in memorable narratives
- Providing behavioral templates
- Enabling multi-generational transmission

**Mechanisms:**
- Simple, relatable scenarios
- Emotional engagement aids retention
- Layers of interpretation (surface vs deeper meaning)
