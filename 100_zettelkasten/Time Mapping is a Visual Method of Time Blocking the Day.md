## Time Mapping is a Visual Method of Time Blocking the Day

**Summary:** Time mapping is a visual and strategic method of breaking down the day into specific time blocks for a wide range of personal and professional responsibilities.

**Details:** Time mapping is a holistic form of time blocking that emphasizes understanding not just what needs to be done, but also when and how time is navigated throughout the day. It often involves tracking the time spent on different activities to improve overall time awareness and achieve a better work-life balance.
