The networking stack works in 'layers'. There are 2 famous models, TCP/IP and OSI. The OSI model has 7 layers and is more fine-grained.

Here are the layers:

- Layer 1: [[Physical Layer]]
- Layer 2: 2b-Data Link Layer
- Layer 3: 2c-Network Layer
- Layer 4: 2d-Transport Layer
- Layer 5: 2e-Session Layer
- Layer 6: 2f-Presentation Layer
- Layer 7: 2g-Application Layer
