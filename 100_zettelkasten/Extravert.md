Thought about how sometimes extroverts come across as less intelligent or less thoughtful. This feeds into the idea of socially validated thinking. If extroverts thinking in public then their thinking is bounded by those around them. They don't spend us much time thinking privately.

- **[[Humans Are Social Creatures]]** - Evolutionary basis for being attuned to others' opinions
- **[[External Validation Reduces Cognitive Dissonance]]** - How external confirmation alleviates psychological discomfort

## Cognitive Effects

- **[[Cognitive Dissonance]]** - The discomfort from conflicting beliefs that social validation helps resolve
