These are the quiet strengths inside you that help you face challenges and stay true to yourself. They are the core components of a strong and resilient spirit.

## Core Resilience Skills

- [[Bravery is Not Shrinking From What is Frightening|Bravery]]: Not shrinking from things that are scary, whether it's trying something new or speaking up for what is right.
- [[Perseverance is Finishing What You Start Despite Difficulty|Perseverance / Grit]]: Finishing what you start, even when it gets difficult. Working hard and not giving up.
- [[Honesty is Being Truthful and Sincere|Honesty]]: Telling the truth, being sincere, and being a person others can trust.
- [[Self-Regulation is Managing Your Emotions and Actions|Self-Regulation]]: Being able to manage your emotions and actions. Thinking before you act.
- [[Prudence is Making Careful Choices for the Future|Prudence]]: Making careful choices and thinking about what might happen in the future before you do something.
- [[Humility is Letting Achievements Speak for Themselves|Humility]]: Being modest and letting your achievements speak for themselves, without showing off.
- [[Resilience is the Ability to Bounce Back from Setbacks|Resilience]]: The ability to bounce back after a setback or disappointment.
