For some individuals with ADHD, voice-to-text dictation can be an easier and more engaging alternative to physical writing. Using a phone or desktop to verbally dictate thoughts, which can then be transcribed automatically, removes the friction of typing or handwriting and allows for a more natural flow of ideas.
