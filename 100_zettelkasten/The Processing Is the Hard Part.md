[[collectors fallacy]]

Taking time to work through those inboxes is harder. Especially when those inboxes are so full. I have too much stuff to go through, and I don't even really know what to do with it.

Trying to express my feelings on the topic is hard. I know what I mean. It is a feeling I have.

What is that feeling?

The feeling/understanding/thought is one thing, the words to describe it are another.

I have the feelings, but not the words.

Because it is so challenging to describe a feeling and I want it to be so accurate and precise, I stumble.

The words never match my understanding, which is why [[Paraphrasing is a Complex Cognitive Skill|paraphrasing is such a complex cognitive skill]].
