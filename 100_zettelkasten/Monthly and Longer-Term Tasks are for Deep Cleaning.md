title: Monthly and Longer-Term Tasks are for Deep Cleaning

Monthly, quarterly, or biannual cleaning tasks are reserved for deep cleaning jobs that are not needed as frequently. Examples include cleaning windows, washing window coverings, deep cleaning appliances, cleaning out closets, and rotating mattresses.
