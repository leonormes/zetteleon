title: Identity-Based Habits are More Resilient Than Goal-Based Habits
type: concept
uid: 
updated: 
---

Habits that are tied to one's identity (e.g., "I am a writer") are more resilient and sustainable than habits that are purely goal-based (e.g., "I will write 500 words a day"). Identity-based habits reframe the behavior as a reflection of who you are, making it more intrinsically motivating.
