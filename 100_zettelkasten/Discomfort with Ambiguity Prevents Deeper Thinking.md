Many people avoid delving deeper into subjects because it requires them to tolerate ambiguity and mental dissonance. The process of questioning initial assumptions and holding conflicting ideas in mind is uncomfortable. This leads them to settle for a surface-level understanding instead of pushing through to a more profound insight.
