Writing affords us the ability to observe our thinking.

We become more conscious of how we structure arguments and make logical leaps

This increased awareness helps us identify and correct weaknesses in our reasoning. This is particularly evident in the act of paraphrasing, which is itself a [[Paraphrasing is a Complex Cognitive Skill|complex cognitive skill]] that forces deep processing.
