## Constructive Debate in Psychological Safety

**Summary:** True psychological safety enables:
- Challenging ideas (not people)
- Disagreeing while respecting
- Resolving over suppressing conflict

**Key Practices:**
- Structured debate formats
- Explicit conflict resolution norms
- Separating ego from ideas

**Communication Tools:**
- [[Active Listening De-escalates Conflict]]
- Structured feedback protocols
- Clear disagreement frameworks
