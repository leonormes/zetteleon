## **Stage 1: The Prima Materia (The Raw Material)**

"Humans are susceptible to contextual myopia, a state where their immediate environment creates a narrow focus bubble. Within this bubble, psychological forces such as the focusing illusion magnify the importance of local tasks and emotions. This can lead to goal displacement, where the procedural means are mistaken for the ultimate ends. The process is self-reinforcing; as individuals invest more ego and effort, the sunk cost fallacy compels them to escalate their commitment, generating artificial justifications for why their current focus is critically important, even when it is objectively trivial."
