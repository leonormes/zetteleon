- **The "Architect's Fallacy":** I assume she needs the *Blueprint* (The Syllabus Structure) before she can lay the first brick.

- **Hypothesis:** She doesn't need a Lecturer; she needs a **Rubber Duck**. She needs me to sit there and ask dumb questions so *she* has to explain the plan to *me*.
